Welcome To the Online Library
Nelson Mandela always believed in education which we truly believe can be obtained through reading. Whether you are commuting or at home, whether you are into short stories or addictive serials, you obtain certain information that wakes or improves your way of seeing and interacting with the world, We created this free online library as young readers honouring Mandela's belief and Legacy. You can find few books on Mandela Public Library, but they will inspire you. "Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world."
